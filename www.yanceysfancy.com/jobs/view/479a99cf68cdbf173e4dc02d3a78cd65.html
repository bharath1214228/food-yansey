Yancey’s Fancy Cheese Company

Remote

Full time

 

Established in 1947 as Kutter’s Cheese, Yancey’s Fancy has evolved throughout its sixty plus year history and developed a reputation for high quality, innovative products. In recent years, Yancy’s Fancy has launched in excess of fifty new and exciting varieties of cheeses that reintroduced the flavors that made New York famous for handcrafted cheddar cheeses many years ago. We are a family-owned company and offer excellent opportunities to thrive and grow.

 

This position requires a leader with an energy level and enthusiasm that aligns with our company’s goals to grow our business nationally and expand across all channels of sales, Retail, Food Service, Mass Merchant, C-Store and Club.

 

The Region Sales Manager is primarily responsible for the growth and development of our sales portfolio in the assigned areas. The Region Sales Manager will work with all Grocery Retailers, Distributors and Wholesalers within that area. He/She is also responsible for all aspects of business management, forecasting, pricing, analytics, trade relations and retail execution. The Region Sales Manager will report to the Vice President of Sales and Marketing. We are looking for an individual to be based in the Western New York market, but with will consider qualified candidates from the Northeast Region.

 

Responsibilities of this Position Include (Duties/Essential Functions)

  • Deliver sales objectives (Distribution/Assortment) across all grocery outlets
  • Grow customer base within the region
  • Foster good working relationships with retail customers and brokers, representing Yancey’s Fancy in a professional manner
  • Consistently visit retail stores (solo/ with brokers) to have a pulse on shelf conditions (opportunities and challenges)
  • Ensure all company policies are being followed
  • Effectively communicate all customer and broker initiatives
  • Plan and coordinate promotions with retail customers and brokers
  • Ownership of the region’s P/L; review on a consistent basis. Taking appropriate action steps to communicate and resolve issues and variances
  • Evaluate and report on analytics. Complete comprehension on Business Insights (IRI), ability to communicate and present effectively

 

 

 

 

 

Required Experience

  • Bachelor’s Degree
  • 5 or more years’ experience in Food Industry Sales
  • 3+ years direst sales leadership or broker leadership experience preferred
  • Proficient MS Office Professional Suite
  • Fully versed in all aspects of syndicated data tools (IRI/Nielsen/Customer Specific tools)
  • Effective time management and scheduling skills with the ability to balance multiple projects simultaneously
  • Self-motivated and the ability to work independently within a large market and multitask across various sales partners
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Willingness to travel (50%)
  • Must have a personal vehicle, a valid driver’s license and excellent driving record
